Espresso Martini

142 Calories | A Dark Elegance

Indulge in the rich, bold flavors of our Espresso Martini. This exquisite blend of premium vodka, creamy Kahlua, and freshly brewed espresso delivers a smooth and invigorating experience, perfect for those who savor the richness of life.

  • Vodka: 50 ml
  • Kahlua: 20 ml
  • Fresh Espresso: 30 ml

Instructions: Brew espresso, shake all ingredients with ice, and strain into a martini glass.

Whiskey Sour

141 Calories | Timeless Sophistication

Experience the harmonious balance of tangy lemon and smooth whiskey in our Whiskey Sour. This classic cocktail offers a sharp yet smooth profile, leaving a memorable sweetness that lingers elegantly on the palate.

  • Whiskey: 50 ml
  • Fresh Lemon Juice: 20 ml
  • Sugar Syrup: 20 ml

Instructions: Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice, and garnish with a cherry or orange peel.

Skinny Bitch

104 Calories | Refined Simplicity

Savor the clean and crisp flavors of our Skinny Bitch. This minimalist cocktail combines premium vodka with sparkling soda water and a hint of fresh lime, offering a light and invigorating sip that is both sophisticated and delightful.

  • Vodka: 50 ml
  • Soda Water: ~100 ml
  • Fresh Lime Juice: 10 ml

Instructions: Combine vodka and lime juice in a tall glass filled with homemade ice, top with soda water, and stir gently.
